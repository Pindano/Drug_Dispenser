Our Mission & Values

Improved Patient Care

MediHelp PLC facilitates better coordination between patients and practitioners, ensuring accurate prescription management and timely access to medications. This enhances the overall quality of healthcare services provided to patients.

Enhanced Efficiency

The automation of various processes, such as patient-practitioner assignment and contract management, streamlines workflow and reduces manual paperwork. This leads to increased operational efficiency and cost savings for healthcare providers.

Transparency and Accountability

By providing a transparent system to track prescription assignments, contract management, and dispatchment confirmations, the project promotes accountability in the medical industry, reducing the risk of errors, fraud, and mismanagement of medications.

Improved Drug Supply Chain

The system's ability to manage contracts and track drug supply instances between pharmacies and pharmaceuticals helps optimize the drug supply chain. This contributes to a more reliable and consistent availability of medications, benefiting both healthcare providers and patients.
